A comprehensive guide for you


There are some key aspects in choosing a phone, which everyone should consider before buying one.

Of course, the first things in mind are the user preferences and the actual purpose of the phone (business, outdoor resistant, etc.)

I have created a list of those things, in the right-hand side of the screen, from most important to least important.

In my opinion, the price should be the last, considering how powerful some cheap phones can get.

What to consider:

  • Build Quality
  • Operating system and support[XDA]
  • Hardware design and features:
    • Processor
    • RAM
    • Camera
    • Audio Quality
    • Battery
    • Storage
  • Size
  • Price

Majority is fine, but don't dive head first, you'll be surprised
